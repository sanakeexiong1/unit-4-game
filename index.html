Crystal Collector

How to Play:

  • At the start of the game, a number has been generated for you. Your job is to match this number using the crystrals.
  • Each crystal has it's own value. As you click, each crystal will add that value to your score. Once your score matches the generated number, you win!
  • Each time you play the game, the value of the crystals will change. Good luck!
